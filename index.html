Image Sweeper

Description

Random Objects were hidden under a field of buttons. Guess the right buttons to clear boxes and uncover the images.

How Game Works:
  • Press the right button, to clear the box and background image will be displayed.
  • Press the wrong button and entire field of boxes will reset back to original.
Rules to Complete the Game:
  • Uncover all the hidden images.
  • Total, Ten Images and Two bonus Images were hidden.
  • Take screenshot of the game field with all uncovered images and upload to google photos using "Upload" button.
Tip:
  • Name the screenshot with a member name in the group.
  • If Game field does not fit in a single screenshot. You can take max two screenshots.
  • You might need to sign in with your google account to upload the screenshot.
