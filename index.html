Image Sweeper

Description

Random Objects were hidden under a field of tiles. Guess the right tiles to uncover the images beneath.

How Game Works:
  • Hit the right Tile, to clear it and background image will be revealed.
  • Hit the wrong Tile and entire field of tiles will reset back to original. Watch your step and Play Smartly.
Rules to Complete the Game:
  • Uncover all the hidden images. Total 15 Images were hidden, with different shapes and sizes.
  • Take screenshot of the game field with all uncovered images and mail to pavan.k.jalla@chase.com
  • Name the screenshot with first letters of all the member names in the group.
Tip:
  • If Game field does not fit in a single screenshot. You can take max two screenshots. You can use browser zoom out.
  • This game can be acceessed outside of client machine as well. Incase if your client machine is slow.
